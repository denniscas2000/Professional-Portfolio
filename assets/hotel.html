Product

Hotel

I developed a user-centric hotel search app as part of a UX/UI project, aiming to streamline the hotel booking experience and alleviate the common challenges users face when navigating through existing hotel apps. The primary goal of the app is to simplify the search process, offering a seamless and intuitive interface that allows users to effortlessly find and book accommodations. By focusing on user experience design, I implemented features that enhance navigation, such as a user-friendly search bar, interactive maps, and personalized recommendations based on individual preferences. The app not only provides a visually appealing design but also prioritizes functionality to ensure users have one less thing to worry about when planning their stay. Through thoughtful UX/UI considerations, the hotel search app aims to revolutionize the way users interact with hotel booking platforms, making the entire process more efficient and enjoyable.

Preface

Research

  1. User Persona Development: Conducted extensive research to create detailed user personas representing the target audience for the hotel search app. This included demographics, behaviors, preferences, and pain points related to hotel booking.
  2. Competitor Analysis: Analyzed existing hotel search and booking apps to identify strengths, weaknesses, and common trends in the user interface. Explored competitor features and functionalities to differentiate and improve upon them.
  3. User Interviews: Engaged in one-on-one interviews with potential users to gather qualitative insights into their hotel booking experiences. Explored user expectations, frustrations, and desires to inform the design process.
  4. Usability Testing: Carried out usability testing sessions with prototypes to observe user interactions and gather feedback on the app's navigational flow, layout, and overall usability. Iterated designs based on user testing insights.
  5. Information Architecture: Developed a solid information architecture by organizing content and features in a logical and intuitive manner. Ensured that users could easily navigate through the app and find relevant information.
  6. Wireframing: Created wireframes to outline the basic structure and layout of the app, focusing on key screens such as the homepage, search results, and booking confirmation. Iteratively refined wireframes based on feedback.
